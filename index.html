Avengers Memorabilia

Fragments of Mjolnir

The unfortunate remains of Mjolnir (RIP) were uncovered by scientists investigating a wormhole appearance in Norway. Warning: These fragments behave very erratically around sour-tempered individuals.

Live Crazy Ants

Discovered snacking on a discarded Mango Fruit Blast near a Baskin Robbins. Very energetic. Warning: Keep away from electronics.

Depleted Arc Reactor

Acquired from an estate sale. Notarized certificates indicate this to be Tony Stark's first working reactor. Warning: Prolonged exposure may result in radiation poisoning.

Hulk's "Slightly" Used Pants

Multiple pairs, worn only once! Disclaimer: Individual items may display differing degrees of fraying.

The Incredible Tooth

Uncovered inside a collapsed high rise during salvage operations in Johannesburg. Certified to be 'human-like' by a medical professional. Weighs 8.1oz!!!

Mk 38 "Igor" Hand

My collection includes numerous fragments from Iron Man's battle with the Mandarin. The largest, most intact piece of Stark armor could be yours! Warning: Contains unexploded ordinance.

Mk 42 Shoulder Piece

Disclaimer: Image not representative of current state. This piece successfully detonated and is mostly unrecognizable. Part was identified via a maker's mark "Mk42 SDR R" on the interior.

Captain America's Prop Shield

Acquired from an Army surplus sale. Own one of the original 4 prop shields carried by Captain America ! Disclaimer: Shield may or may not have seen combat.

Widow's Bite Module

You could own a disposable energy module from one of the most electric Avengers ever! The electric battery used to discharge up to 30,000 volts is mostly depleted, but I wouldn't suggest touching your tongue with it.

Thor's Armor

Dislodged pieces, not the whole thing. Fragments of unknown origin from his scale mail were found in cleanup efforts following the alien attack on Greenwich.

Vibranium Axe Head

This rare surprise comes from a friend after destroying the main axle of a scrap metal shredder. He is selling the axe head to replace and upgrade the critically damaged machine.

Spider-Man's Web Hammock

My very first purchase from the Department of Damage Control's inventory liquidation! Disclaimer: Not responsible for any folding or crumpling during shipping.

On a budget? Buy some battle memorabilia!

Grand Central Station Exit

My very first foray into collecting, acquired in New York while working for the newly formed Department of Damage Control. I couldn't keep any alien tech, but I passed this sign every day on my way to work.

Greenwich Books

Books recovered after the alien attack in Greenwich. All items recovered have a legible library seal from the Royal Naval College for authenticity.

Sokovian Real Estate

After being relocated from the city center to the lake shore, this fixer-upper's original owner opted to sell instead of rebuild. The land mass extends beyond the original lake shore, so the has approved this new real estate!

Triskelion Turntable

A turntable found in the rubble after a "Shield" (Hydra) Helicarrier crashed into the Triskelion. It probably belonged to some high ranking officer, since it was found among the upper floors.

And for you weirdos who like the bad guys...

"Piece" of Mind

A piece of tape from the data drives that housed the consciousness of Hydra's Dr. Zola. Data mining efforts have concluded that this piece of tape corresponds with the mad doctor's sense of smell.

Alien Gun (shell only)

The external shell from one of thousands of alien guns used in the New York invasion. The Department of Damage Control began selling the outer shell after gutting the inner electronics. The most evil doorstop you could ever desire.

Alien Poop

Totally not a week's worth of stinkies collected from my pet Pekingese, although you totally deserve it if you buy anything here. Free with any other evil memorabilia! We'll even drive it out personally and light it on fire!
